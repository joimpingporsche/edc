Definition: Organization Organizations represent the users/owners of Applications or other business objetcts that can be modeled within different dimensions to create hierarchical structures. In Porsche AG's LeanIX, we distinguish between the elements of the structural organization and the elements of the process organization according to SAFe.
The elements of the organizational structure represent the organizational structure of Porsche AG and its subsidiaries. The process organization is defined by the SAFe organization and is divided into portfolios and ARTs.
Purpose and benefits By using the organizational units, it is possible to describe and define the areas responsible and used for an object. This information is relevant for necessary evaluations and analyses, as well as the identification of the respective responsible areas in the event of problems and changes.
